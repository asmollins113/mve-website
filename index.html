Who We Are

The Most Valuable
Experience
in the Room

It started with three Rollins students who shared a passion for music and a simple belief that Orlando deserved better nights. What began as an idea became Most Valuable Entertainment: an Orlando-based event production, promotion, and booking company built from the ground up by people who actually love the culture.

We curate the city's premier nightlife experiences, bringing the community together through music, energy, and unforgettable moments. Whether we're producing our own events or promoting the best nights Orlando has to offer, we don't just fill a room. We create spaces where every person feels like the most valuable attendee, from the DJ booth to the dance floor.
